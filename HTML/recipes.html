Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 T Wired Brain Coffee Hot Cocoa Mix
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Marshmllows (optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop Hot Cocoa Mix into a mug.
  2. Heat milk on the stove or in the microwave to just below a boil
  3. Pour milk into the mug, stirring gently as you pour.
  4. Stir until mix is fully dissolved in the milk.
  5. Top with marshmallow if you must.

Cocoa Facts

Do you know the difference?
Cocoa
Cocoa is the powdered substance left after processing cacao beans and has little or no cacao butter.
Chocolate
Chocolate is basically cocoa with the cacao butter and often suger and milk.
